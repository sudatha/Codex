## Role Description

Infosys is seeking a dynamic, strategic, and innovative **Data Architect / Senior Data Modeler - Azure Synapse / Data Vault** to support the growth of the Data and Analytics business unit.

The role includes:
- Coaching client teams through business transformation using data and AI.
- Leading and contributing to strategic master planning and advisory engagements.
- Supporting execution and delivery of transformation initiatives in a leadership capacity.
- Representing Infosys thought leadership in public forums and media.

## Location and Compensation

- Location: Melbourne
- Salary: AUD 134,132 - 140,193 annual gross
- Flexible working arrangements: Hybrid (case-by-case)

## Roles and Responsibilities

- Design and develop conceptual, logical, and physical enterprise data models.
- Design and implement enterprise data warehouse models using:
  - Data Vault 2.0
  - Dimensional modelling
  - Normalised EDW models (3NF)
- Enable full auditability, lineage, historical tracking, and extensibility using Data Vault practices.
- Design and implement optimized data models for Azure Synapse Analytics.
- Design and maintain enterprise-grade data models in Teradata EDW.
- Optimize model performance using partitioning, distribution strategies (hash, round robin), and indexing.
- Model banking subject areas including party/customer, accounts, transactions, payments, risk, regulatory reporting, and finance.
- Ensure models comply with enterprise data governance, metadata, and lineage standards.

## Essential Skills

- 12+ years of experience in Data Engineering, including 4+ years as Data Modeler / Lead BA.
- Strong hands-on experience in:
  - Dimensional modelling
  - EDW modelling (3NF)
  - Data Vault 2.0
- Strong experience with Azure Synapse, ADLS Gen2, and ADF.
- Strong SQL expertise in Azure Synapse SQL and Teradata SQL.
- Experience with modelling tools such as Erwin.
- Strong banking domain experience, including core banking, regulatory reporting, risk, and finance.
