# Job Description - NAB Migration Architect / Solution Designer

## About The Job
If you like making complex change feel safe, simple and repeatable, this is the role. In NAB's Customer Migration Team, the role designs standards and guardrails that stop migrations becoming bespoke one-offs, so each wave runs predictably, is evidence-led, and stands up to scrutiny.

Join the Customer Migration Team at NAB and build control standards that make migration events safe and repeatable, including validation, reconciliation, lineage, and exceptions.

The role also focuses on:
- Turning complex integration and non-functional decisions into clear guardrails teams can execute.
- Baking traceability and auditability into designs so approvals are faster and evidence is clean.

## Team And Culture
NAB states that its people are customer obsessed and prioritize customer outcomes above all else. The organization highlights innovation, strong customer relationships, and a goal to be customer centric across Australia and New Zealand.

## Role Context
Role title: Migration Architect / Solution Designer
Team: Customer Migration (Enterprise Simplification)

## Day-To-Day Responsibilities
- Set standards for validations, reconciliations, lineage, and exception flows.
- Make integration and non-functional guardrail decisions across orchestration, pipelines, and reporting.
- Codify rollback patterns and enable dry-run and rehearsal.
- Maintain a reusable pattern library and run design reviews.
- Maintain traceability from design decision to runbook step to evidence artifact.

## Required Experience And Capabilities
- Background in solution design or solution architecture, with extensive architecture experience in regulated environments (financial services preferred).
- Practical control engineering mindset with designs that are testable and auditable.
- Experience translating architecture into runbooks, gates, and evidence.
- Ability to align Risk, Legal, and Tech quickly on "safe to proceed" decisions.
