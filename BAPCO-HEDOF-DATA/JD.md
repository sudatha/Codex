# Bapcor - Head of Business Intelligence

## About Bapcor
Bapcor Ltd is the leading automotive aftermarket provider of accessories, equipment and services, with brands including Burson, Autobarn, Midas, Truckline, Premier Auto Trade and Baxters.

Bapcor's Melbourne-based corporate team supports strategy and execution across more than 1,000 locations and over 5,000 team members in Australia, New Zealand and Thailand.

Core values:

- Being in it together
- Giving a damn

## About the Role
Reporting to the CTO and serving as a member of the Technology Leadership Team, the Head of Business Intelligence leads Bapcor's enterprise data and analytics agenda.

This role sets strategy, leads capability uplift, and builds trusted partnerships across business and technology teams. It also embeds a customer-centred, insight-driven culture across Trade, Retail, Specialist Wholesale and the commercial truck parts portfolio.

The role is central to modernising legacy data systems, establishing scalable data foundations, unlocking commercial and customer value through BI, and driving reporting innovation through AI and strategic vendor partnerships.

## What You Will Lead and Deliver
- Set and drive enterprise BI strategy and roadmap aligned to business priorities and long-term technology direction.
- Lead design, development and optimisation of data platforms, pipelines, modelling, analytics products, dashboards and reporting.
- Oversee data governance, quality and standards to ensure accuracy, accessibility, compliance and consistency.
- Partner with executives, senior leaders and operational teams to translate business needs into actionable insights.
- Champion a data-driven culture, uplift capability, enable self-service analytics and promote data literacy.
- Simplify and modernise legacy data systems to improve scalability, reliability and efficiency.
- Lead BI teams and external partners, building capability and value through strong vendor and stakeholder management.
- Contribute to enterprise technology strategy, investment and decision making through Technology Leadership Team participation.

## What You Will Bring
- 15+ years in IT with deep expertise across Data Analytics, BI, Data Science, Program Delivery and IT Business Engagement in large, complex environments.
- Proven leadership building, coaching and developing high-performing technical and analytical teams.
- Strategic enterprise mindset and ability to simplify complexity and influence senior stakeholders.
- Strong communication skills that connect technical concepts to business outcomes.
- Demonstrated experience modernising data platforms with strong data governance and data quality practices, ideally including Azure Data Platforms.
- Commercial and financial acumen including budget management, vendor management and commercial negotiation.
- Strong project management, prioritisation and execution capability in fast-paced environments.
- Customer-focused, results-driven and adaptable leadership with continuous improvement mindset.
