# Senior BI Developer / Power BI Specialist (Contract)

A well-regarded Victorian Government agency is seeking a contract Senior BI Developer / Power BI Specialist to deliver migration-focused reporting across a live legacy-to-Azure platform transition. The role is hands-on, covering dashboard development, data shaping, and trusted reporting for progress, outcomes, and operational performance.

This is a highly stakeholder-facing role requiring strong verbal and written communication, engagement, and presentation capability.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design and deliver Power BI dashboards and reporting to support migration and operational reporting needs.
- Lead requirements workshops and translate business needs into clear metrics, measures, and visuals.
- Build and maintain data models (semantic models), DAX measures, and Power BI visuals that drive decision-making.
- Produce and maintain single source of truth reporting, including KPI definitions and consistent measurement.
- Work closely with data and technical teams to validate sources, reconcile results, and improve data quality.
- Present dashboards and insights to stakeholders; explain assumptions, data limitations, and recommendations.
- Support continuous improvement across reporting standards, usability, and performance.

## Required Skills and Experience
- Proven senior experience delivering end-to-end Power BI solutions (requirements to build, publish, and iterate).
- Strong SQL / SQL Server capability (T-SQL, querying, joins, shaping data for reporting).
- Strong experience with data modelling and reporting best practices (performance, governance, usability).
- Excellent stakeholder engagement; able to influence and challenge constructively.
- Confident presenter who can communicate clearly with senior stakeholders.

## Nice to Have
- Australian Public Service, Victorian Public Service, or consulting company experience.
- Migration/transformation program reporting exposure.
- Data transformation experience (Power Query, data cleansing, modelling).
- Exposure to documentation/design inputs (e.g., Synops, Axure) that feed reporting requirements.

## Personal Attributes
- Delivery-focused, proactive, and accountable.
- Friendly, personable, open, sharing, and team-focused.
- Strong judgement around metrics and measurement.
- Comfortable in ambiguity and fast-moving environments.
