# Senior Analytics Consultant

## What We're Looking For

We are seeking a Senior Analytics Consultant to join our Data Practice. The role needs a visual storyteller who can turn complex data into actionable insights in a high-visibility, stakeholder-facing environment.

## You Might Work On

- Design and develop high-impact, executive-level Power BI dashboards and reports that drive business value.
- Architect robust, scalable semantic models using DAX and Power Query (M) to maintain a single source of truth.
- Lead workshops and presentations, translating ambiguous business questions into technical requirements and clear visual solutions.
- Collaborate with Data Engineers to optimize data structures for front-end performance and user experience (UX).
- Define visualization best practices, governance frameworks, and self-service analytics strategies for enterprise clients.

## Ideally You'll Have

- Expert-level Power BI including advanced DAX, Paginated Reports, and Power BI Service administration.
- Strong SQL for data profiling and strong understanding of star schema and dimensional modeling (Kimball).
- Exceptional boardroom presence and the ability to explain complex data trends to non-technical stakeholders.
- Proven consulting mindset with requirement gathering, dashboard UI/UX principles, and client expectation management.
- Experience with the Fabric/Power BI ecosystem and cloud integrations such as Azure Synapse, Snowflake, or Databricks.
