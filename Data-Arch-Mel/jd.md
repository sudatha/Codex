# Job Description

## Data Architect - Melbourne (Long-Term Contract)

- Location: Melbourne (Hybrid)
- Contract: 6-12 months

## Role Overview

We are currently looking for an experienced Data Architect for a long-term contract opportunity based in Melbourne with a hybrid work model.

This is an exciting opportunity for someone who enjoys taking ownership and driving architectural direction, governance, and documentation standards.

## What We Are Looking For

- Strong core Data Architecture background (not Data Engineering focused)
- Proven experience working in enterprise environments
- Excellent documentation and governance experience
- Ability to drive and lead architectural functions
- Strong stakeholder management skills
- Experience in Financial Services is highly advantageous
- Comfortable working in a hybrid setup in Melbourne

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead and drive the existing data architecture function
- Define and maintain enterprise data architecture frameworks
- Ensure high-quality documentation, standards, and governance
- Collaborate with business and technical stakeholders
- Provide architectural guidance across data platforms and initiatives
