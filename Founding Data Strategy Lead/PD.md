# Position Description (PD)

## Position Title
Founding Commercial Strategy Lead

## Business Context
This role sits within a newly established, distinct business entity focused on improving how people in Australia navigate healthcare. The organisation is in a greenfield phase, building strategy, operating model, and team from the ground up.

## Reporting Line
- Reports to: Head of Data
- Role level: Foundational leadership role, acting as second-in-command (2IC) within the Commercial Strategy and Analytics function

## Role Purpose
Establish and lead the Commercial Strategy and Analytics capability from zero. Define and execute the commercial data playbook, set the multi-year strategic direction, and create a scalable foundation for future team growth.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design and implement the commercial data and analytics strategy from the ground up.
- Translate health strategy and policy direction into actionable, data-driven commercial initiatives.
- Develop and own a 3-year roadmap for using data to improve commercial outcomes and customer health navigation.
- Partner with senior leadership to align priorities, influence decisions, and secure investment.
- Build strong cross-functional relationships across finance, clinical, and product teams.
- Define the operating model and growth plan for a future analytics and data team.

## Stakeholder Engagement
- Senior leadership and executive decision-makers
- Finance teams
- Clinical teams
- Product teams
- Data and analytics partners

## Core Deliverables
- Commercial data and analytics playbook
- 3-year strategic roadmap
- Prioritised portfolio of data-driven commercial initiatives
- Cross-functional alignment and investment case development
- Team design and capability growth plan

## Success Profile
- Strategic and entrepreneurial mindset with comfort in ambiguity
- Ability to build functions, frameworks, and teams from first principles
- Strong commercial thinking supported by analytics
- Excellent influence and partnership capability across diverse stakeholders
- Leadership potential to scale into a larger team leadership remit

## Value Proposition of the Role
- Rare founder-style opportunity to build a critical function from day one
- High organisational impact with clear outcomes in Australian healthcare
- Startup-like ownership with backing, stability, and resources of a major corporate
- Clear path toward building and leading a significant team
