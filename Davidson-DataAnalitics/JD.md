# Davidson - Data & Analytics Specialist

## About the Company
Davidson is partnering with a major bank to bring a Data and Analytics consultant to sit within the retail banking space. They're investing in sharper reporting and decision-making, and need someone who can turn data into insight that leaders actually use.

## About the Role
This Data & Analytics Specialist contract sits within a retail banking environment, focusing on Power BI reporting and SQL-driven analysis. You'll work closely with business owners and stakeholders to understand what they need, translate that into meaningful reporting, and present insights clearly and confidently.

## Key Responsibilities
- Build and enhance Power BI dashboards and reports for retail banking stakeholders.
- Write and optimise SQL queries to extract, validate, and transform data for reporting.
- Produce actionable insights and trends to support business decision-making.
- Present reporting outcomes and insights to business owners and stakeholders.
- Ensure reporting is accurate, consistent, and aligned to business definitions and metrics.
- Support continuous improvement of reporting processes, data quality, and usability.

## About You
- Strong, hands-on Power BI capability including data modelling, DAX, visuals, and report optimisation.
- Strong SQL skills including complex querying, joins, aggregation, and performance considerations.
- Proven experience in retail banking.
- Confidence engaging with and presenting to business owners and stakeholders.
- Experience working within banking or financial services.
