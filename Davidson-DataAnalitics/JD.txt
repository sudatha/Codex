About the Role
This Data & Analytics Specialist contract sits within a retail banking environment, focusing on Power BI reporting and SQL-driven analysis. You'll work closely with business owners and stakeholders to understand what they need, translate that into meaningful reporting, and present insights clearly and confidently.

Key responsibilities include:

Building and enhancing Power BI dashboards and reports for retail banking stakeholders
Writing and optimising SQL queries to extract, validate and transform data for reporting
Producing actionable insights and trends to support business decision-making
Presenting reporting outcomes and insights to business owners and stakeholders
Ensuring reporting is accurate, consistent, and aligned to business definitions and metrics
Supporting continuous improvement of reporting processes, data quality, and usability
About You

Strong, hands-on Power BI capability (data modelling, DAX, visuals, report optimisation)
Strong SQL skills (complex querying, joins, aggregation, performance considerations)
Proven experience in retail banking (this is essential)
Confidence engaging with and presenting to business owners/stakeholders
