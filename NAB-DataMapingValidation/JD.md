# Consultant - Data Mapping and Validation (NAB)

## About the Role
This role focuses on turning complex, messy migration data into clean, testable, and audit-ready outcomes.

The Consultant - Data Mapping and Validation in the Customer Migration team is responsible for producing precise mapping and validation artefacts so migration cutovers are predictable and approvals are faster.

## Key Focus Areas
- Own mapping specifications and rulebooks that are testable, controlled, and evidence-ready.
- Convert data quality findings into concrete validation rules and exception handling.
- Build artefacts and checks that improve migration confidence and reduce cutover risk.

## Day-to-Day Responsibilities
- Profile source data, document structures, and define mapping rules including edge cases.
- Define validations and reconciliations and support SIT/UAT with defect triage.
- Produce pre-load and post-load evidence artefacts.
- Feed runbook steps and readiness gates.
- Maintain mapping accuracy and artefact quality under time pressure.

## What NAB Is Looking For
- Strong BA and data analysis fundamentals.
- Disciplined documentation.
- Comfort with validations, reconciliations, exception flows, and test support.
- Ability to work with architects, engineers, and product teams to resolve edge cases quickly.
- A bias for evidence quality and audit cleanliness, not just delivery speed.

## Culture
NAB emphasizes customer obsession, inclusion, equity, and respect. The environment supports flexible ways of working and values diverse backgrounds and identities.
