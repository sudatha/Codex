# Data, Analytics & Insights Consultant

## Role Overview
A Data, Analytics & Insights Consultant is required to gather, define, and lead the design of data and reporting solutions for a well-known financial services business.

## Key Responsibilities
- Partner with business leaders to gather data and reporting requirements.
- Design and deliver insightful and actionable visualisation solutions.
- Liaise with data governance and engineering teams to ensure data solutions are scalable.
- Act as an SME in selecting and implementing relevant data and business modelling techniques.
- Implement and drive adoption of data management procedures.
- Lead implementation of advanced analytical solutions.

## Required Skills and Experience
- Extensive data analytics background.
- Strong business intelligence and visualisation capability.
- Experience in transformation or change management environments.
- Proven ability to gather, define, and translate business requirements into analytical solutions.
- Strong background with Databricks and Power BI.
- Financial services experience is highly beneficial.

## Application Notes
- Apply via the advertised application process or email resume to the listed contact.
- Applicants should confirm right to work in Australia.
- Expected salary and years of relevant experience will be requested during application.
