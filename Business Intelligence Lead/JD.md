
We are working on a brand new Business Intelligence Lead role where you will be defining what best-in-class data looks like and elevating analytics capability across the organisation.

This is a hands-on individual contributor role responsible for leading the technical direction, shaping strategy, and helping to evolve capability & ways of working.

## Responsibilities
- Designing and implementing semantic models and reusable patterns to provide a consistent, governed, and business-friendly layer for reporting and analytics
- Collaborating with business stakeholders to align definitions, calculations, and reporting logic, creating a single source of truth
- Helping establish best practices within the team across BI architecture, dimensional modelling and visualisation
- Coaching & mentoring on the people side (no direct reports)
- Change management - driving continuous business innovation & evolution by keeping up-to-date with the latest Data & AI technologies

## What We Are Looking For
- Someone with a good degree of seniority who is still close to the technology, this is very much a hands-on enabling role
- Experience building Power BI dashboards & reports
- Good knowledge of data modelling & design
- Knowledge of modern data platforms and cloud ecosystems
- Leadership experience (architecture/strategy)
- Proactive mindset, someone comfortable with ambiguity who can use their initiative
- Excellent communication skills
