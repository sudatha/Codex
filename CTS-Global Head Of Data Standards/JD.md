# Job Description

## Global Head of Data Standards
**Company:** Corporate Travel Management (CTM)  
**Type:** Permanent (Hybrid)

## About CTM
CTM is a global, award-winning provider of travel management solutions for corporate, events, leisure, and loyalty markets. CTM operates across New Zealand, Australia, Asia, North America, and Europe with more than 3,000 employees.

CTM values are:
- Connect
- Deliver
- Evolve

## About the Role
The purpose of this role is to provide leadership, oversight, and governance to design, govern, and oversee implementation of data standards and integrity across CTM's global and regional operations.

The role ensures data used for decision-making, reporting, and customer engagement is consistent and trusted. It establishes and maintains frameworks, standards, and controls that protect CTM's data assets and align with internal assurance requirements.

As the global authority for data standards, the role promotes accountability and continuous improvement in data management practices. It integrates inputs across Account Management, Sales, Customer, Operations, and Finance to enable:
- exceptional client insights
- commercial modelling
- operational forecasting
- supplier strategy
- monetisation and productisation of CTM's data assets

## Key Responsibilities
### Establish and Maintain Enterprise Data Frameworks
- Design and oversee enterprise-wide data standards, policies, business rules, and control frameworks.
- Safeguard data accuracy and integrity across global systems and processes.
- Ensure standards reflect multi-domain inputs and operational, commercial, and customer-facing requirements.

### Provide Strategic Leadership and Oversight
- Lead global data standards and control programs.
- Strengthen governance maturity and drive continuous improvement.
- Ensure standards support innovation, platform convergence, and enterprise-wide insight generation.

### Promote Effective Communication and Engagement
- Communicate policies and insights across global and regional teams.
- Embed consistent understanding and accountability.
- Translate complex data dependencies into clear business impacts for Sales, Account Management, Customer, and Operations leaders.

### Report on Data Standards and Integrity
- Provide transparent reporting to executive leadership on:
  - data standard performance
  - control effectiveness
  - risk mitigation
- Highlight commercial, client, and operational impacts linked to data quality uplift.

### Drive Behavioural Change
- Champion data stewardship and continuous improvement culture.
- Influence behaviours, align practices, and foster shared ownership of data accuracy.

### Enable Commercial and Client-Facing Value
- Ensure data standards support CTM's ability to:
  - deliver premium customer insights
  - enhance supplier negotiations
  - improve forecasting accuracy
  - create new data-driven products and propositions
