# Silverchain - Data Architect (Job Description)

A unique opportunity to join Silverchain is available as the organisation progresses its digital ambitions. Reporting to the Digital Program Technical Lead, the Data Architect plays a strategic role in shaping the data foundations of Silverchain's Digital Program, a multi-year transformation delivering a national operational care platform powered by Microsoft Dynamics 365.

This role ensures program data is structured, secure, integrated, and aligned with business goals, leveraging the Microsoft ecosystem to enable analytics, reporting, and AI-driven insights.

## What's On Offer

- Full-time, one-year fixed term role (Monday-Friday, 76 hours per fortnight)
- Hybrid role with work-from-home flexibility across VIC, WA, SA, or NSW
- Salary packaging up to $18,500 tax free (non-profit benefits)
- Everyday discounts for major retailers
- Comprehensive health and wellbeing support

## What Silverchain Is Looking For

- Tertiary qualifications in Computer Science, Data, Information Technology, or related discipline
- Proven experience as a Data Architect or Enterprise Architect in large-scale enterprise or government programs
- Strong understanding of enterprise architecture frameworks, with TOGAF or equivalent certification
- Deep expertise in Azure data services:
  - Microsoft Fabric
  - Azure Data Factory
  - Azure Synapse Analytics
  - Azure Data Lake Storage
  - Azure SQL / Cosmos DB
- Experience with Dynamics 365 CE (Dataverse) and Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations, including data models, integration, and reporting
- Proficiency in Power Platform (Power BI, Power Automate, Power Apps)

## Key Responsibilities

- Define and maintain enterprise data architecture aligned with TOGAF principles and business objectives
- Establish data strategy and roadmap for integrating Dynamics 365 CE and F&O with Azure data services and LOB systems
- Design and document data models, integration patterns, and reference architectures across Dataverse, ADF, Fabric, Synapse, and related services
- Create robust integration strategies between Dynamics 365 CE/F&O, third-party systems, and external data sources
- Enforce data governance frameworks, metadata management, data lineage, and data quality standards
- Establish controls for security, privacy, and access management across enterprise data assets
- Work with business stakeholders to turn requirements into scalable data solutions
- Collaborate with solution architects, application teams, and infrastructure specialists to ensure design and integration alignment

## Why Choose Silverchain

Silverchain is a leading Australian home care provider accredited to national health and aged care standards. As a not-for-profit organisation, Silverchain reinvests funds into research and innovative care models, and supports employee growth through training and education.

Silverchain values a diverse workforce and encourages applications from Indigenous Australians, people of all ages, genders, abilities, and culturally and linguistically diverse backgrounds.
