## Key Responsibilities

### ETL Development

Design and develop ETL processes to extract, transform, and load data from multiple sources into data warehouses or data lakes.

Optimize ETL workflows for performance, scalability, and reliability.

### Data Pipeline Management

Build and maintain data pipelines that ingest and process structured and unstructured data.

Monitor and troubleshoot data pipelines to ensure data quality and availability.

### Data Modeling

Collaborate with database architects and data analysts to create effective data models that support reporting and analytics.

Implement best practices for data storage and retrieval.

### Data Integration

Integrate third-party APIs and other data sources to enrich data sets.

Ensure seamless data flow between different systems and applications.

### Collaboration

Work closely with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and understand business needs.

Communicate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders about data initiatives.

### Documentation

Maintain clear documentation for ETL processes, data pipelines, and data models.

Provide training and support to team members on data-related tools and processes.

### Quality Assurance

Perform data validation and quality checks to ensure data integrity and accuracy.

Implement monitoring and alerting solutions for ETL processes.
