# Data Architect - iSelect

Location: Auckland

## About The Role
iSelect is seeking a Data Architect to shape the data ecosystem across iSelect and NZHL. The role focuses on designing scalable data architecture solutions that support business growth and critical data initiatives, while partnering across business and technology to define architecture standards, improve data governance, and enable high-quality data for decision-making.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead the design and implementation of data architecture solutions aligned with business objectives and technology strategy
- Develop and maintain conceptual, logical, and physical data models and data flows
- Establish and enforce data standards, policies, and best practices
- Collaborate with analysts, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand data requirements and guide solution design
- Oversee integration of disparate systems and ensure data quality, consistency, and security
- Evaluate existing data architecture and recommend improvements for scalability, efficiency, and maintainability
- Support cloud-based data platforms, data warehouses, and modern data tooling

## What They Are Looking For
- Proven experience as a Data Architect or Senior Data Engineer / Data Modeler
- Expertise in data modeling, data warehousing, and database design
- Strong knowledge of ETL/ELT, integration patterns, and APIs
- Experience with cloud data platforms and modern data stacks
- Solid SQL capability and understanding of governance, lineage, security, and data quality frameworks
- Ability to translate business requirements into practical, scalable data solutions
- Strong stakeholder management and communication skills
