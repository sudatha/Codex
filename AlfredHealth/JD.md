# Job Description - Clinical Registry Technical Lead (Bayside Health)

## About The Organisation
Bayside Health is a public health service delivering care across metropolitan Melbourne, the Mornington Peninsula, Koo Wee Rup, Bass Coast, and Southern Gippsland. It supports around 1.2 million people through hospitals, centres, and clinics, with more than 22,000 staff.

Bayside Health was formed on 1 January 2026 through the merger of Alfred Health, Bass Coast Health, Gippsland Southern Health Service, Kooweerup Regional Health Service, and Peninsula Health.

## Employment Details
- Fixed term: 12 months

- Classification: Managers and Administrative Workers Grade 6
- Location: Alfred Health

## Department Context
The School of Public Health and Preventive Medicine (Monash University) focuses on improving health systems, informing policy, and generating impactful evidence.

The Clinical Outcomes Data Reporting and Research Program (CORRP) manages several national clinical registries, including:
- Australian Breast Device Registry (ABDR)
- Australian Diabetes Clinical Quality Registry (ADCQR)

The ADCQR is a national registry in development, built on foundations from the Australian National Diabetes Audit (ANDA), with a focus on monitoring quality and effectiveness of diabetes care.

## Role Summary
This is a multifunctional technical role requiring capability across backend systems, frontend development, and advanced analytics/reporting. The role collaborates across multiple clinical quality registry teams, including ADCQR and ABDR.
