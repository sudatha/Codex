# Job Description

## About the Role

We are currently seeking an innovative, curious, and enthusiastic Data Engineer to join our growing Data & Technology team, as we seek to harness data-driven strategy to drive performance both on and off the field.

This is a unique opportunity to combine your technical expertise with the passion and energy of working in an elite sporting organisation. You will play a pivotal role in shaping the Club's data ecosystem, liaising with key stakeholders across the Club to build the infrastructure and pipelines that empower smarter, faster, and more informed decision-making.

The role also carries an educational component, championing best practices in data collection, secure storage, and governance, while guiding colleagues in understanding the importance of safe, responsible data use.

Continual innovation is a hallmark of the Club's Data & Technology team. This role will be provided the opportunity to proactively explore emerging technologies, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and creative problem-solving methods to keep Carlton at the cutting edge of sports analytics.

## Key Responsibilities

- Build and maintain scalable ETL/ELT data pipelines that connect diverse sources to the Club's centralised data platform.
- Oversee and optimise the Club's Snowflake data warehouse, including facilitation, integration, and delivery of usable data to stakeholders across all functions.
- Drive automation of key business functions using Python, SQL, Power Automate, and other tools to create streamlined and efficient workflows, reducing manual effort and improving productivity.
- Implement robust data governance strategies to ensure the accuracy, usability, and accessibility of key datasets, while upholding data security best practices and maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements.
- Collaborate with key stakeholders across all teams, providing support on data-related technical challenges, while educating colleagues on safe, secure data practices and responsible AI usage.
- Develop and maintain clear, comprehensive documentation to guide the Club's data users in leveraging new data sources and instructing on best practices.
- Stay at the forefront of emerging technologies and AI, regularly researching trends, tools, and practices that could enhance the Club's operations and performance.
- Contribute to the evaluation and development of AI-driven proof of concepts, helping transform innovative ideas into practical solutions.
- Collaborate with senior team members to identify opportunities for AI integration, supporting the implementation of innovative solutions to enhance productivity and decision-making across departments.
- Participate in pilot projects and technology trials, gathering feedback and documenting outcomes to inform future adoption of new tools and platforms.
- Share insights and findings with colleagues, helping to foster a culture of continuous learning and innovation around emerging technologies and AI within the Club.

## Experience, Skills and Attributes

- Undergraduate degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science, or any related field and/or equivalent experience.
- 2+ years of previous work experience in data engineering or analytical role(s) (or similar).
- Previous experience working in a sporting organisation is preferred, although not essential.
- Experience building and maintaining automated data pipelines from flat file and API-based sources, using ETL/ELT tools (Matillion, Fivetran, etc.), SQL, relevant programming languages including Python, and cloud platforms.
- Experience working with and administering data warehouses, preferably Snowflake.
- Experience with AWS cloud services and tools, such as RDS, EC2, and S3 buckets.
- Familiarity with both Mac and Windows operating systems.
- Proficiency with industry data quality standards (cleansing, validation, and governance).
- Proficiency in creating and maintaining data documentation and version control systems.
- Experience working with business intelligence/visualisation tools (e.g. Tableau, Power BI).
- Familiarity with Atlassian products (e.g. Confluence, Jira).
- Strong communication skills and an ability to build positive working relationships with others.
- Curious, innovative thinker with a highly developed analytical ability and strong problem-solving skills.
- Highly self-motivated and proactive, with an ability to work both autonomously and collaboratively as part of a team.
- Effective time management and stakeholder management skills, with a proven ability to manage multiple competing priorities.
