## The Role and Team

As a Data Engineer within the ANZ Data team, you will play a key role in transforming business requirements into practical, data-driven solutions. You will be responsible for migrating and shaping data, building views that empower teams across the organization, and ensuring data is accessible and valuable for decision-making.

Your primary focus will be data integrity and security. You will lead the analysis, design, and development of tools and frameworks that support data transformation, migration, and other critical services.

A key part of the role involves supporting and migrating legacy data systems, which remain essential to success. By creating repeatable and reliable processes, you will ensure data solutions are robust, scalable, and aligned with business needs.

## What We Are Looking For

- Extensive Data Engineer experience with strong hands-on expertise in Azure Databricks, including advanced SQL and Python for data transformation and migration (essential)
- Proven ability to build strong cross-departmental relationships across the ANZ region, leveraging business insight and technical expertise to provide strategic advice and improved outcomes (essential)
- Strong technical capability with a solid understanding of big data platforms and architecture
- Proficiency in data development tools and ETL processes
- Exceptional communication skills, with the ability to explain technical and business processes clearly to stakeholders at all levels
- Relevant certifications in Azure Databricks or Azure Data Engineering (preferred)
