# DJ Homes Interview Training Pack

This pack is built from:
- `Key Selection Criteria Questions.docx`
- `PD.md`
- `CV.docx`

## Files in this pack
- `01-Scoring-Strategy.md`: What the panel is scoring and how to maximize marks.
- `02-KSC-Model-Answers.md`: High-quality model answers for all 8 criteria questions.
- `03-Mock-Interview-Drills.md`: Practice questions, follow-ups, and a rehearsal plan.

## How to use this pack
1. Read `01-Scoring-Strategy.md` once before every practice session.
2. Rehearse each answer in `02-KSC-Model-Answers.md` out loud in 90-120 seconds.
3. Use `03-Mock-Interview-Drills.md` for timed simulation and gap-fixing.
4. Replace any placeholder metrics with your exact numbers before interview day.
