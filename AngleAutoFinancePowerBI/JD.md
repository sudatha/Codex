Purpose;



The Senior Reporting Analyst is responsible for designing, building, and delivering high-quality enterprise reporting and semantic models, with a strong focus on Power BI, business-facing analytics, and end-to-end delivery ownership.



This is a senior, lead-level individual contributor role that provides technical & delivery leadership across the reporting and analytics domain. The role sits at the intersection of business intelligence, data modelling, and delivery execution, and plays a key role in shaping reporting standards, patterns, and priorities across multiple initiatives.



You will work closely with business stakeholders to translate reporting requirements into robust and scalable semantic models and intuitive Power BI reports, while partnering with data engineering to ensure alignment with upstream data models (Snowflake / dbt). The role is expected to oversee and coordinate reporting initiatives, provide guidance and quality assurance across concurrent deliveries, and act as a trusted point of escalation for complex reporting and modelling challenges.



The role requires strong hands-on Power BI capability, solid SQL and data modelling skills, and the ability to manage reporting deliverables through to production in a structured, reliable manner, while setting best-practice expectations for performance, governance, and usability across the enterprise reporting landscape.





Key Responsibilities / Deliverables;



Power BI Reporting & Semantic Modelling

Design and build high quality Power BI reports and dashboards aligned to business and executive use cases.

Develop and maintain Power BI semantic models (Import, DirectQuery, or Composite) with a strong focus on performance, usability, and governance.

Create robust DAX measures following best-practice modelling and performance patterns.

Ensure consistency of metrics, definitions, and calculations across reports.



Data Modelling & Analytics Enablement

Design analytical data models optimised for reporting and self-service analytics.

Work closely with data engineering teams to influence and consume Snowflake and dbt models.

Translate complex transactional and financial data into business-friendly analytical structures.



Delivery & Stakeholder Management

Own reporting initiatives end-to-end, from requirements gathering through to production release.

Partner with business stakeholders to clarify requirements, manage scope, and prioritise delivery.

Coordinate delivery timelines, dependencies, and release planning for reporting assets.

Support UAT, stakeholder sign-off, and post-deployment refinement.



Platform & Operational Excellence

Support the ongoing reliability, performance, and usability of the Power BI platform.

Investigate and resolve production issues related to data accuracy, refresh failures, or performance.

Contribute to governance standards including naming conventions, workspace design, deployment processes, and access controls.

Assist in re-engineering legacy reports and models to improve performance, maintainability, and scalability.





Qualifications;

Relevant business, quantitative or IT related degree or certifications will be an advantage





Experience;

Proven experience delivering enterprise Power BI reports and semantic models in a production environment.

Strong proficiency in Power BI, including data modelling, DAX, performance optimisation, and workspace management.

Strong SQL skills for data analysis and reporting use cases.

Solid understanding of data modelling concepts (star schemas, facts, dimensions, grain, surrogate keys).

Exposure to Snowflake or similar cloud data warehouse platforms.

Familiarity with Agile or iterative delivery approaches.

Demonstrated ability to manage reporting deliverables through to completion with minimal supervision.

Microsoft Certified: Power BI Data Analyst Associate or equivalent
