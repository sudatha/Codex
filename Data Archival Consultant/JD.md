# Technical Data Archival Consultant

## Location and Work Model
- Melbourne, Australia
- Hybrid work model
- Full-time, permanent
- Australian Citizen or Permanent Resident

## Role Overview
Join a global, mission-driven team focused on patient safety through secure and compliant healthcare data archiving. The role supports legacy data transition projects for healthcare providers across Australia.

## Key Responsibilities
- Perform analysis and discovery on legacy EHR, Patient Accounting, and ERP systems.
- Collaborate with client stakeholders to understand legacy workflows and data requirements.
- Extract, transform, and load data into an Azure-hosted archival platform.
- Work with ETL and software engineers across varied data formats and workflows.
- Ensure data integrity and accuracy through QA and data validation.
- Manage support tickets independently and provide strong customer service.
- Help providers retain compliant and accessible records after system transition.

## Required Skills and Attributes
- Strong SQL skills (intermediate to advanced).
- Excellent written and verbal communication.
- Strong analytical and troubleshooting capability.
- Self-starter mindset with strong problem-solving focus.
- Ability to work independently and manage priorities.

## Desirable Experience
- Clinical EHR systems.
- Patient financial systems.
- Non-patient systems such as HR, Payroll, GL, AP, and MMIS.

## Why Join
- Contribute to improving healthcare safety globally.
- Work with modern cloud-based data archiving technology.
- Supportive and collaborative team culture.
- Growth opportunities in a high-demand domain.
- Flexible hybrid working.
- Open to candidates from graduate level through 1-5 years' experience.
