# Reporting & Data Analyst - Job Description

## Engagement
- Contract until end of September
- CBD location with hybrid working arrangement
- Government department

## Role Purpose
A highly analytical and detail-driven Reporting & Data Analyst is required to transform complex security data into clear executive insights that support informed business decisions.

## What You Will Do
- Design and deliver clear, concise executive and Board-level cyber security reports and dashboards in Power BI.
- Consolidate and synthesise data from multiple security platforms into a centralised, trusted reporting view.
- Build and maintain scalable ETL/ELT data pipelines for analytics and reporting.
- Develop an annual Security Report for Schools, translating technical metrics into a clear narrative on trends, risks, and performance.
- Analyse security data to identify patterns, insights, and actionable recommendations for senior stakeholders.

## What You Will Bring
- Strong experience as a Reporting & Data Analyst.
- Strong Power BI experience: dashboard design, data modelling, DAX, and visual storytelling.
- Experience designing and maintaining data pipelines and ETL/ELT processes.
- Strong data modelling and data consolidation capability.
- Ability to translate complex technical information into clear, non-technical insights.
