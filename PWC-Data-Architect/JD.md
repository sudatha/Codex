# PwC Australia - Director, Data & AI (Solutions Architect)

## About the Role
At PwC Australia, the Data & AI team is expanding and seeking an experienced Solutions Architect to design and deliver innovative data solutions that create measurable business value. The role sits at the intersection of business and technology, translating complex requirements into scalable, enterprise-grade data architectures.

## Why PwC
- Opportunity to solve complex industry challenges across finance, technology, energy, and health.
- Work with leading teams and emerging technologies.
- Clear career progression with continuous learning and leadership development.
- Flexible working arrangements.
- Comprehensive leave and benefits including parental leave.

## What You Will Do
### 1) Design and Architect Data Solutions
- Design enterprise data architectures across data lakes, data warehouses, lakehouse platforms, and real-time analytics systems.
- Ensure scalability, performance, and security in solution designs.
- Produce solution blueprints, technical designs, and architecture decision records.
- Guide implementation teams and align architecture to business objectives and technical standards.

### 2) Drive Revenue and Delivery Impact
- Lead end-to-end sales and delivery of high-value data solutions.
- Own revenue targets and delivery profitability across a client portfolio.
- Oversee the full delivery lifecycle: architecture, implementation roadmaps, sprint execution, QA, and client acceptance.
- Manage multiple concurrent enterprise data engagements.

### 3) Stakeholder Engagement
- Collaborate with client stakeholders to align solutions with business needs.
- Bridge business requirements and technical implementation.
- Communicate complex data concepts to non-technical audiences.
- Validate technical feasibility of proposed solutions.

### 4) Best Practices and Innovation
- Establish architecture standards, governance frameworks, and engineering best practices.
- Drive data quality, security, and maintainability.
- Evaluate and adopt emerging data technologies and methodologies.
