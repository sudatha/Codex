# Senior Data Engineer

## Company Description
Bendigo Kangan Institute (BKI) is one of Victoria's largest TAFE providers, offering modern training centres and a broad range of education and skills pathways.

## Role Details
- **Location:** Broadmeadows or Cremorne, with regular travel to other BKI sites
- **Role Type:** Full-time, permanent
- **Remuneration:** From $164,669.64 including super

## About the Role
The Senior Data Engineer is a hands-on leadership role responsible for designing, implementing, and optimising BKI's enterprise data ecosystem.

The role shapes the Central Data Platform, aligns delivery with Enterprise Data Architecture principles, and leads end-to-end engineering of robust and scalable data pipelines.

The position partners with ICT, Performance and Reporting, and business stakeholders to uplift data governance, improve data quality, and enable advanced analytics and insights.

The role also mentors two Data Engineers and embeds best-practice DevOps/DataOps, automation, CI/CD, observability, and cloud-native engineering.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead design and development of enterprise data models: conceptual, logical, and physical.
- Develop and enhance scalable data pipelines across APIs, databases, and third-party systems.
- Ensure high-quality data ingestion, monitoring, and orchestration into the Enterprise Data Platform.
- Drive enterprise-wide data governance maturity, including metadata, lineage, cataloguing, and classification.
- Embed DevOps/DataOps practices including CI/CD automation, observability, and platform lifecycle management.
- Translate business requirements into robust engineering solutions.
- Drive continuous improvement, performance optimisation, and alignment to strategic objectives.

## Required Skills and Experience
- Hands-on Azure cloud data services including:
  - Azure Databricks
  - Azure Data Lake Storage
  - Azure SQL / Synapse
  - Event Grid, Event Hub, Functions
  - Azure DevOps, Git, CI/CD
  - ARM / Bicep for Infrastructure as Code
  - Microsoft Fabric (desirable)
- Strong data governance and metadata management capability (Purview desirable).
- Structured analytical problem-solving.
- Strong stakeholder engagement and ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical audiences.
- Experience leading teams or mentoring engineers.
- Experience in education or regulated environments (desirable).
